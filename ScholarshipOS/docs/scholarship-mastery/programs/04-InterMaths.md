# InterMaths — Erasmus Mundus Joint Master in Interdisciplinary Mathematics

## Overview
A 2-year (120 ECTS) program in **mathematical modelling and simulation** with interdisciplinary applications in biomedical sciences and industrial engineering. The most direct match for a **Statistics** undergraduate.

| University | Location |

| Autonomous University of Barcelona (UAB) | Barcelona, Spain |
| University of Côte d'Azur (UniCA) | Nice, France |

- Stochastics for modelling and simulation
- Applied and industrial mathematics
- Mathematical modelling in biomedical sciences
- Mathematical modelling in engineering
- Others depending on host university

## Scholarship
| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly stipend | €1,400 (24 months = €33,600) |
| Tuition fees | Fully covered (participation costs: €18,000 total for 2 years) |
| 3 scholarships reserved for targeted regions | Bangladesh qualifies for these |

## Eligibility
- Bachelor's degree in **Mathematics, Statistics, Physics, Engineering, or related field**
- Strong mathematical background
- English proficiency (B2 level)
- No separate scholarship application — applying by deadline = considered for scholarship

## Application Process

### Step 1 — Verify Math Prerequisites (Jun–Sep 2026)
Your Statistics degree covers calculus, linear algebra, probability, and statistical modelling — all prerequisites met.

### Step 2 — Prepare Documents (Sep 2026 – Feb 2027)
- **Europass CV** emphasizing statistics, mathematical modelling, ML
- **Motivation letter** (explain interest in interdisciplinary mathematics)
- **Certified transcripts** + degree certificate (English translation)
- **English proficiency** (IELTS 6.0+ / TOEFL 80+)
- **2 reference letters** (at least 1 from a math/statistics professor)
- **Passport copy**
- Reference letters can be submitted **after** the deadline

### Step 3 — Submit Application (Nov 2026 – Feb 2027)
- Apply on the InterMaths online portal
- Deadline typically **late February**
- No application fee

### Step 4 — Selection (Mar–Apr 2027)
- Evaluation committee ranks all applicants based on academic merit, motivation, and background
- Ranking determines scholarship allocation

### Step 5 — Results (Apr–May 2027)
- Main list, reserve list, and consortium scholarship candidates notified
- Accept offer and confirm enrollment

### Step 6 — Visa & Pre-departure (May–Aug 2027)
- Italian student visa (coordinating university)
- Housing, travel arrangements

### Step 7 — Start (Sep 2027)
- Program begins at University of L'Aquila
