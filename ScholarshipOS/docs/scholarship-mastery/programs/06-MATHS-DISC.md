# MATHS-DISC — Mathematical Modelling, Simulation and Data Science for Industrial and Societal Challenges

## Overview
A 2-year (120 ECTS) Erasmus Mundus joint master combining **mathematical modelling, simulation, data science, and AI** to solve complex industrial and societal challenges. Features 8 specialization pathways.

## Why It Fits You
- Specifically designed for **Statistics, Mathematics, Physics, Engineering, CS, Economics** graduates
- Requires **6 ECTS Statistics** + 18 ECTS Calculus + 6 ECTS Programming — your Statistics degree covers these
- **Scientific Machine Learning** specialization is directly relevant to your ML career goals
- Your Kaggle notebooks and EDA projects demonstrate applied data science skills
- 2 application rounds (main + mid-year) — more flexibility
- Strong PhD preparation pathway

## Eligibility
- BSc in Mathematics, Statistics, Physics, Engineering, CS, Economics with strong math component
- **CGPA > 75%** — check your 3.25/4.00 equivalent
- Prerequisites: Calculus (18 ECTS), Linear Algebra (6 ECTS), Numerical Analysis (6 ECTS), Programming (6 ECTS), **Statistics (6 ECTS)**
- English: IELTS 6.5+ / TOEFL iBT 80+ / Cambridge B2 First 173+
- Must graduate by 31 July (non-EU) before program starts

## Application Process
1. Apply via MATHS-DISC online portal at https://www.mathsdisc.eu/
2. Choose your specialization pathway preference
3. Submit: transcripts, degree, CGPA proof, English proof, Europass CV, motivation letter, study plan
4. Interview may be conducted
5. Results announced after deadline
