# EMAI — Erasmus Mundus Joint Master in Artificial Intelligence

## Overview
A 2-year (120 ECTS) joint master's degree integrating **machine learning with modern symbolic AI** across theory, methods, and applications. Ranked among the top Erasmus Mundus AI programs.

## Eligibility
- Bachelor's degree with **180 ECTS minimum**
- At least **60 ECTS** in ICT, Engineering, Physics, **Mathematics, Statistics**, or equivalent
- Specific credit requirements:
  - 12 ECTS in CS/CE (cryptography, networks, theory)
  - 12 ECTS in programming (algorithms, data structures)
  - 12 ECTS in mathematics (calculus, linear algebra, logic, **probability & statistics**, discrete math)
- English proficiency (B2/C1 typically required)
- **Your Statistics degree + ML coursework covers these requirements**

## Application Process

### Step 1 — Check Eligibility (Jun–Sep 2026)
Verify your transcripts meet the 12+12+12 ECTS credit thresholds. Your Statistics degree with programming electives is likely sufficient.

### Step 2 — Prepare Documents (Sep–Nov 2026)
- **CV** in Europass format
- **Motivation letter** (tailored to EMAI — emphasize AI/ML projects)
- **Academic transcripts** + degree certificate (certified English translation)
- **English proficiency** (IELTS 6.5+ / TOEFL 90+ recommended)
- **2 recommendation letters** (academic or professional)
- **Passport copy**

### Step 3 — Submit Application (Oct–Dec 2026)
- Apply via the EMAI online portal (typically opens October, deadline ~December 20)
- No separate scholarship application — submitting by the deadline auto-considers you

### Step 4 — Selection & Interview (Feb–Apr 2027)
- Committee evaluates: academic merit, motivation, recommendation letters, background relevance
- Shortlisted candidates may be interviewed

### Step 5 — Results (Apr–May 2027)
- Main list or reserve list notification
- Accept offer within 3–5 days if selected

### Step 6 — Visa & Pre-departure (Jun–Aug 2027)
- Apply for Spanish student visa (first country)
- Arrange accommodation
- Attend pre-departure orientation

### Step 7 — Start (Sep 2027)
- First semester begins in Barcelona
