# Other Fully Funded Scholarships — Beyond Erasmus Mundus

While you prepare for Erasmus Mundus (Oct 2026 – Feb 2027 deadlines), also consider these **alternative fully funded options** for your ML/AI/Statistics profile.

---

## 1. MBZUAI — Mohamed Bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence (UAE)

### Overview
World's first dedicated AI university in Abu Dhabi. All admitted MSc students receive **full scholarships automatically**.

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Location** | Abu Dhabi, UAE |
| **Programs** | MSc in ML, Computer Vision, NLP, Robotics |
| **Duration** | 2 years |
| **Scholarship** | 100% tuition + **15,500 AED/mo stipend** (~€3,900/mo) + housing + visa + health insurance |
| **Deadline** | Rolling (apply anytime, but earlier = better) |
| **Requirements** | BSc in STEM, CGPA 3.2+ (yours: 3.25 ✅), English proof, GRE optional |
| **Fit for you** | **95%** — Direct ML focus, generous stipend, AI-only university |

### Why apply?
- Highest stipend of any option (~€3,900/month vs Erasmus €1,400/month)
- AI-exclusive university — perfect for ML career
- No separate scholarship application (admission = scholarship)
- **Apply now** (rolling admissions)

### How to Apply
1. Visit https://mbzuai.ac.ae/
2. Submit online application
3. Take online screening exam (math, statistics, Python, ML fundamentals)
4. Technical interview
5. Admission decision + scholarship offer

## 2. DAAD Scholarships (Germany)

### Overview
German government-funded scholarships for developing countries (Bangladesh qualifies). Study at any German public university (tuition-free).

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Location** | Germany (any public university) |
| **Programs** | Any MSc in ML, Data Science, AI, Statistics |
| **Duration** | 12–24 months |
| **Scholarship** | €934/month + health insurance + travel + study allowance |
| **Deadline** | Varies by program (Aug–Sep for some, earlier for others) |
| **Requirements** | BSc, 2+ years work experience (for EPOS), English/German |
| **Fit for you** | **85%** — Germany is tuition-free + living stipend |

### Best Programs for You
- **Data Science** at TU Munich, TU Berlin, Uni Bonn
- **ML/AI** at TU Munich, TU Darmstadt, Saarland
- **Statistics** at LMU Munich, HU Berlin, Uni Göttingen
- **Computational Science** at TU Berlin, FAU Erlangen

### How to Apply
1. Search DAAD database: https://www.daad.de/en/ → filter by Bangladesh + Master's
2. Find a program that matches your profile
3. Apply directly to the program + DAAD scholarship simultaneously
4. Submit: CV, motivation letter, transcripts, English proof, research proposal (if PhD), work experience proof

### DAAD EPOS (Development-Related)
- Requires **2 years professional experience**
- €934/month + insurance + travel
- Deadlines: usually **August–September** for following year
- Your ML Engineer role counts toward the 2-year requirement

## 3. Fulbright Foreign Student Program (USA)

### Overview
Flagship US government scholarship for Bangladeshi students to study Master's in the USA.

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Location** | USA (any US university) |
| **Duration** | 1–2 years |
| **Scholarship** | Full tuition + **$1,800–$2,500/month stipend** + airfare + health insurance |
| **Deadline** | ~11 July 2026 (for 2027/28 cycle) |
| **Requirements** | Bangladeshi citizen, 4-year Bachelor or 3-year + Master, **2+ years work experience**, strong leadership, return to Bangladesh after |
| **Fit for you** | **80%** — Needs work experience, your ML role + leadership count |

### Why apply?
- Prestige — most competitive US scholarship for Bangladeshis
- Full coverage — one of the most generous packages
- Leadership focus — your Project Manager + Volunteer experience helps

### How to Apply
1. Visit https://bd.usembassy.gov/ → Education → Fulbright
2. Submit online application by July deadline
3. Documents: transcripts, CV, essays, English proof, recommendation letters
4. Interview: if shortlisted (usually September–October)
5. Results: early next year

## 4. Chevening Scholarships (UK)

### Overview
UK Government's global scholarship for future leaders. Fully funded 1-year Master's at any UK university.

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Location** | UK (any UK university) |
| **Duration** | 1 year |
| **Scholarship** | Full tuition + monthly stipend + travel + arrival allowance + visa |
| **Deadline** | ~October–November 2026 (for 2027/28) |
| **Requirements** | Bangladeshi citizen, **2,800 hours work experience**, leadership potential, return to Bangladesh after |
| **Fit for you** | **75%** — Needs 2,800 hrs work; your ML + leadership roles may qualify |

### How to Apply
1. Visit https://www.chevening.org/
2. Choose up to 3 UK university programs
3. Submit: CV, essays (4 questions: leadership, networking, study plan, return plan), English proof, recommendation letters
4. Interview: if shortlisted (usually February–March)
5. Results: June–July

### Best UK Programs for You
- **MSc Machine Learning** — UCL, Imperial, Edinburgh
- **MSc Data Science** — UCL, Warwick, Bristol
- **MSc Statistics** — Oxford, Cambridge, LSE, Imperial
- **MSc Artificial Intelligence** — Imperial, Edinburgh, King's College
